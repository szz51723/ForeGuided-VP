# ForeGuided-VP
This repository is the code of "A Scenario-Driven Method for Predictive Real-World Bus Energy Optimization".

## Abstract
Efficient electric buses energy management is essential for urban decarbonization. Existing predictive energy optimization strategies rely on static fitting of historical time-series data. With the assumption of identical data distributions, these still struggle with the non-stationary real-world operation dynamics. To overcome this, we propose a novel scenario-driven predictive framework guided by an Implicit Spatio-Temporal Information Graph (IG). Rather than merely mapping past statistics, we try to decipher and integrate the underlying evolutionary logic of complex traffic scenarios. The IG modulates a knowledge distillation process. It adaptively enforces the Inferencer model to adhere to historical physical laws during stable conditions, while selectively absorbing the proactive priors of the Guidance model to anticipate traffic deviations during sudden anomalies. By coupling deep scenario awareness with forward-reasoning mechanisms, the research transitions battery management from passive data fitting to active evolution inference, highlighting the promise of a transformative technical paradigm for sustainable transportation.
